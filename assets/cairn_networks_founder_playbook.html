Section 03

The three legged stool

One master agent is fragile. Three gives you coverage, price leverage, and arbitrage. Shop every deal through all three.

Avant Communications

Primary

Deep enterprise. AI and cloud focused. Pathfinder assessment tool is a differentiator. 150+ carriers. Your existing relationship carries over.

Move: Migrate your agent code to independent status the week you resign. Zero friction.

Telarus

Secondary

Largest channel sales org. SEs at no charge. 300+ providers. Strong in UCaaS, SASE, cloud. Best agent tools.

Move: Apply week 2. Fast track for experienced reps is standard.

Sandler Partners

Third leg

Family owned. 200+ providers. Sometimes highest first year commissions in the industry. Strong on regional carriers.

Move: Shop every deal through all three. Pick the winner. 10-20% uplift.

Ten plays to a million

Execute seven of ten at quality between now and October 31. You clear a million in combined cash and enterprise value.

PLAY 01
Warm book blitz
$250k to $500k
Days 1 to 45

Fifteen plus active relationships across Brandon and Alanna. Call every one the week your Spectrum badge deactivates. Frame: "We are independent now. We can shop any carrier. What is on your roadmap?" Identify three to five deals in the next 90 days. Barry-Wehmiller multi-site is the obvious first. Belleview second. Post Holdings and Lockton follow.

3 deals avg $40k MRC = $120k MRC. At 2.5x SPIF = $300k. Plus year one residuals at 6% = $86k.
PLAY 02
The migration play
$200k to $400k
Days 30 to 180

Every mid market enterprise is migrating MPLS to SD-WAN or SASE. You know this because you were selling it. Position Cairn as the carrier agnostic advisor. "Let me evaluate all carriers against your current spend." Pricing comparisons close themselves.

4 migrations avg $25k MRC = $100k MRC. At 2x SPIF = $200k upfront. Year one residuals $72k.
PLAY 03
The whale hunt
$300k to $700k
Days 60 to 180

Three logos with $100k+ MRC potential. AI infrastructure, neocloud, GPU data centers. CoreWeave style. Lambda Labs. Applied Digital. High bandwidth, high value, high SPIF. One whale closes and the quarter is made.

1 whale at $120k MRC. Triple SPIF promo window = $360k upfront. Residuals $86k year one.
PLAY 04
The product stack
2x revenue per logo
Ongoing

Never sell one product. Sell voice, data, security, mobility, cloud. Every enterprise has all five. Close one, expand the rest. UCaaS seats at $200-500 SPIF each. 2,000 seats = $400k to $1M SPIF alone.

Average deal size goes from $20k MRC to $50k MRC. SPIFs stack across products. Per logo uplift 2.5x.
PLAY 05
WBE certification
Fortune 500 access
Days 30 to 90

Alanna is majority owner. Apply for WBENC certification. Most Fortune 500s have diversity supplier spend targets. Gives Cairn preferred vendor access to companies that could not hear you otherwise. Competitive moat that does not exist without this structure.

Opens 5 to 10 enterprise accounts otherwise inaccessible. Each worth $100k to $500k ARR.
PLAY 06
Master agent arbitrage
10-20% uplift
Every deal

Shop every deal through Avant, Telarus, and Sandler. Ask each for their best commission on the specific carrier and product. Pick the winner. Same customer, same service, 10-20 percent more in your pocket. Free money for thirty minutes of email.

On $500k annual SPIFs, arbitrage adds $50k to $100k. On $200k residuals, adds $20k to $40k.
