## Offer Letter Text

**[Date]**

Dear [Candidate Name],

We are pleased to offer you the position of **Business Development Representative** at Signal Advisory LLC.

Signal Advisory is an independent technology advisory firm based in Kansas City, Missouri. We help businesses make better technology decisions — connectivity, cloud, security, and mobility. You will be the first voice our prospects hear, and that role matters to us.

Here are the details of your offer.

---

**Position:** Business Development Representative
**Reports to:** Brandon Murphy, Principal Advisor
**Location:** Fully remote
**Start date:** On or around July 1, 2026
**Hours:** Monday through Friday, aligned to US Central Time business hours (8:00 AM – 5:00 PM CT)

---

**Compensation**

Your base pay is **$[X]/month**, paid in USD on the 1st of each calendar month via Wise or Deel.

You are eligible for a **performance bonus** based on qualified meetings booked and confirmed each month:

- 30 to 49 qualified meetings: $25 per meeting above 30
- 50 or more qualified meetings: $50 per meeting above 50

A qualified meeting is defined as a confirmed calendar appointment with a decision maker or key influencer at a target company, where at least two BANT criteria have been confirmed and the meeting is logged in HubSpot prior to the appointment.

Your target is 50 qualified meetings per month. At that level, your total monthly compensation would be approximately **$[base + bonus]**.

---

**What we expect**

- 60 outbound dials per day
- 15 live conversations per day
- 2 qualified meetings booked per day
- 10 follow-up emails per day
- 5 LinkedIn connection requests per day
- CRM (HubSpot) updated daily — every call logged, every outcome tagged
- One Loom video summary sent to Brandon every Friday

---

**What you can expect from us**

- Clear targets with no moving goalposts
- Payment on time, every month, no exceptions
- Direct feedback — we will tell you what is working and what is not
- A shot at growing with the company as we scale

---

**Contractor status**

This is an independent contractor engagement. You are responsible for your own taxes in your country of residence. Signal Advisory will not withhold taxes on your behalf. You will receive a summary of payments made at the end of each calendar year if required.

---

**Confidentiality**

By accepting this offer, you agree to keep Signal Advisory's client lists, prospect information, pricing, and business strategy confidential during and after your engagement.

---

**To accept this offer**, please reply to this message confirming your acceptance and your preferred payment method (Wise or Deel). We will follow up with onboarding details, tool access, and your first week schedule.

We are excited to have you on the team.

- **Adjust base pay** based on candidate location: Philippines $1,800–2,200/mo; Colombia $2,200–2,800/mo; South Africa $2,000–2,500/mo.
