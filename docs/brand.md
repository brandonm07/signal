## Voice

Pulled from the BDR playbook. Applies to every surface — site, email,
proposal, cold call.

**Cairn sounds like a person. Not a brand. Not a corporation. Not a script.**

What it sounds like:
- **Conversational.** Like talking to a neighbor over the fence.
- **Direct.** We tell the truth quickly.
- **Confident without pushy.** We know the value. We also know when to back off.
- **Human.** We acknowledge the awkwardness of cold calling. We treat people like people.
- **Curious.** We ask more than we tell.

What it does not sound like:
- Corporate jargon (synergies, solutions, optimized outcomes).
- Aggressive or high pressure.
- Robotic or scripted.
- Slick.

**The golden rule.** If what you are about to say would make you cringe if
your mom heard it, do not say it.

## Typography (seed)

- **Display — Fraunces.** Warm variable serif. Headlines, quotes, moments
  you want the reader to slow down. Weights 300, 500.
- **Body — Manrope.** Clean, modern, engineered for screens. Weights 400,
  600.

Both are free on Google Fonts. Easy to swap later.
